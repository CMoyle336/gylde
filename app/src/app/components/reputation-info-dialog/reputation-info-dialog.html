What is Reputation?

Your reputation score reflects how you interact with others on Gylde. It determines your daily messaging limits and who you can connect with. As your reputation grows, you'll unlock more features and reach.

Reputation Tiers

New Just getting started. Limited daily messages.
Active Engaged member. Increased messaging capacity.
Established Trusted presence. Can message anyone.
Trusted Highly trusted. Generous messaging limits.
Distinguished Exemplary member. Maximum privileges.
