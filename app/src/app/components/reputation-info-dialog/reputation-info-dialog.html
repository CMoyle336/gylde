What is Reputation?

Your reputation score reflects how you interact with others on Gylde. It determines your daily messaging limits, photo uploads, and who you can connect with. As your reputation grows, you'll unlock more features and reach.

Reputation Tiers

Tier Messages/Day Photos Reach
New 5 3 Limited
Active 15 5 Limited
Established 30 8 Anyone
Trusted 50 12 Anyone
Distinguished 100 15 Anyone

star Premium subscribers get 20 photos regardless of reputation tier.
