What is Reputation?

Your reputation score reflects how you interact with others on Gylde. It determines how many new conversations you can start with higher-tier members each day, and how many photos you can upload.

How Messaging Works

  • chat Existing conversations: Unlimited messages with anyone you're already talking to
  • group Same or lower tier: Start unlimited new conversations
  • arrow_upward Higher tier: Limited new conversations per day (see table below)

Reputation Tiers

Tier Higher Tier Conversations Photos

What Helps Your Reputation

  • check_circle Completing your profile with photos and details
  • check_circle Verifying your identity and email
  • check_circle Having meaningful conversations
  • check_circle Being an active, consistent member
  • check_circle Receiving favorites and matches

What Hurts Your Reputation

  • cancel Being blocked by other members
  • cancel Receiving reports from other users
  • cancel Having an incomplete profile
  • cancel Sending many messages without responses
